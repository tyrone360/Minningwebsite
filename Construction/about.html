Our story

MinePulse began as a passion for rare minerals and a vision for a cinematic online mine shop.

Founded in 2020

MinePulse launched in the summer of 2020, rooted in a small warehouse near the quartz ridges. From the start, we wanted a destination that blended discovery and delivery.

Built on trust

We source minerals from verified quarry partners, then prepare each order with a focus on quality, packaging, and a seamless checkout flow.

Designed for miners

Every product, from volcanic crystals to meteor stones, is selected to support explorers, builders, and collectors who want both beauty and performance.

MinePulse isn’t just a shop — it’s a visual mining brand. We combine deep-earth sourcing, modern digital design, and a clear logistics promise: your order will arrive with the same care it left the vault.

With every page, we share more than products. We share the journey from quarry to crate, the delivery details that matter, and the proof that MinePulse is built on real locations and real inventory.
